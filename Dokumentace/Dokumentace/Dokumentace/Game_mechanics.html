Description of Game Mechanics

Talisman has some game mechanics easy to understand:

  • Objective:The goal is to get to the top of the game board trough stages of the game with talisman in their inventory. To get to higher stage player must defeat Sentinel on sentinel section.
  • Movement: Movement is determined with dice throw. To throw a dice player must press spacebar. Players can move whatever direction they can with arrows on keyboard. For example rolling 3 on dice means player can move 3 spaces to direction they can.
  • Sections:The game board has few sections. On sections like "Forest", "Graveyard", "Cracks", "ElvForest", and "Ruins" player draws a card by pressing button D on keyboard. On sections like "Village", "City", "Tavern" and "Chapel" player can choose to enter and make desired action on popup window. And seciton "Sentinel" player can choose to fight sentinel to enter next section.
  • Drawing card:Card can be drawn by pressing D on keyboard. If it is and enemy fight will occure. If it is "Object Card", "Magic Object" or "Follower" player can take the card to their deck, giving them various stat boosts
  • Fight:If fight occures player rolls dice for enemy, then rolls for himself and then presses space again to show fight result. If player looses they loose life.
  • Popup windows:When player performs various action listed above it shows popup window. To close a popup window player needs to perform action like throw a dice or move on the game board. They can press End turn and leave the game to the next player.
  • Stats:Each character has different stats that can be enhanced in game. Stats are used in fights or in some board sections.
